他手合浜(たてごうはま)

Introduction

The so-called 他手合浜(たてごうはま) refers to the scene where Mt. Fuji overlaps with Mt. Omuro in front. Mt. Omuro is surrounded by Mt. Fuji behind. It looks like Mt. Fuji holding a child. It is a very affectionate scene under the camera.

Address：山梨県富士河口湖町精進483
