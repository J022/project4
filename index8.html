Introduction

"Akafuji" refers to the beautiful scenery of Mount Fuji with a red background under the morning sun or the setting sun in the west.

emergence time of Akafuji

From late summer to early autumn, you can only see this beautiful scenery when you have fresh air and high-altitude clouds that reflect red light. Therefore, "Red Fuji" is a very rare natural phenomenon, so it has become a summer attraction.
