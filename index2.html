長池親水公園(ながいけしんすいこうえん)

Introduction

ながいけしんすいこうえん is the largest public park near やまなかこ(山中湖). It has a complete parking lot, can accommodate many vehicles, and has steps directly to the lake to play in the water. The classic picture here is Inverse Fuji and Diamond Fuji.
