Introduction

"Diamond Fuji" is a unique and rare landscape. It only appears in a few specific places. On a clear and cloudless day with a steady airflow, the track of the setting sun coincides with the top of Mount Fuji, shining brightly, as if it were set with a huge diamond.

the appearance time of Diamond fuji

Diamond Fuji has a naturally occurring cycle called "Diamond Fuji Week", which begins in early February and ends in mid-February
